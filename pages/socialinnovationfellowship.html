Social Innovation Fellowship

Fellowship

Led in partnership with the award-winning Social Enterprise Greenhouse (SEG), the Social Innovation Fellowship is a two-year developmental program where SEG staff provide training, coaching, and hands-on social innovation experience. In addition to instruction, hands-on training, and mentorship, fellows are provided with an annual stipend of $2,000.

In year one, fellows participate in regular workshops led by SEG. In year two, fellows have the option to (1) develop and launch a startup of their own or, (2) complete a substantial project within a SEG-supported social venture.

Pointers

  • Create your own startup
  • Receive training, mentorship, and a stipend
  • Learn and meet those involved in the SEG network
