Venture Fund

Van Wickle Ventures (VWV) routinely backs Brown and RISD’s groundbreaking students, faculty, and alumni on their ventures. An evergreen fund, VWV’s investment gains are invested back into the fund, ensuring that it remains a permanent supporter of Brown University’s entrepreneurial ecosystem.
